Welcome to the allergen friendly food finder website! To get started, you can look in the restaurant tab for a list of available restaurant options. Once you find your desired restaurant, head over to the Allergen Finder tab to look at a map for the location of the restaurant. Then, use the filter options to look for available food options for your specific allergy. Finally, you are able to enjoy your meal at the restaurant!
